# Darken — Privacy Policy

**Source:** https://github.com/lokomaster1/darken

## Summary

Darken is a free, libre, open-source screen dimming overlay for Android. It does **NOT** collect, sell, or share personal data. The app has **NO Internet permission** and CANNOT send data off your device.

## Data collection

Darken does NOT collect analytics, advertising identifiers, location, contacts, or any personal information. There are no accounts, crash reporters, or monetization SDKs.

## Data stored on your device

Settings are saved locally only (default dimming level, filter color, language, auto-start, privacy acknowledgement). Data stays in the app’s private storage and is not transmitted.


## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| Display over other apps | Draw the dimming overlay |
| Notifications (Android 13+) | Minimal foreground-service icon while dimming runs |
| Battery optimization (optional) | Reduce risk of the system stopping the service |

You can review or revoke permissions in Android system settings.

## Third-party libraries

Standard open-source Android libraries (AndroidX, Jetpack Compose) are bundled in the APK and run locally. Darken does not use them to collect data and does not use any proprietary libraries
